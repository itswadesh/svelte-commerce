# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary: Shoppers looking for a fast, modern online shopping experience.
Secondary: Developers and merchants looking for a high-performance, customizable e-commerce storefront.

## Product Purpose
Svelte Commerce is an open-source, headless e-commerce frontend built with SvelteKit and Tailwind CSS. It aims to provide a "lightning-fast" shopping experience that is easy to deploy and connect to any backend API (Litekart, Medusa, Shopify, etc.).

## Brand Personality
- **Performant**: Everything feels fast and lightweight.
- **Modern**: Uses the latest web standards and design patterns.
- **Clean**: A minimalist aesthetic that focuses on product photography and easy navigation.

## Anti-references
- **Clunky Enterprise**: Overly complex, slow-loading pages with excessive tracking and popups.
- **Generic Templates**: Uninspired, "bootstrap-flavored" layouts that lack character.
- **The SaaS Cliché**: Neon gradients on dark backgrounds or overly playful "startup" illustrations.

## Design Principles
1. **Speed as a Feature**: Beyond just load times, every transition and interaction must feel snappy.
2. **Product as Hero**: The interface should recede to allow product imagery to dominate the visual hierarchy.
3. **Frictionless Flow**: Minimize the steps from discovery to checkout. Use progressive disclosure for complex options.
4. **Mobile First, Not Mobile Only**: Ensure a tailored experience for touch that scales gracefully to desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- Support for keyboard navigation throughout the shopping funnel.
- Clear focus states and semantic HTML.
